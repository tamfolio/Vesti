What is the Vesti Card?

The Vesti card lets you spend using the real exchange rate online, in
-store and while you travel - but exists only in your Wise account, either
on your phone or laptop.

There’s no need to carry your wallet, and no risk of your card being
compromised. Your Vesti card has different details to your physical card,
and can be frozen after each purchase. That means it’s a safe, smart and
simple way to spend no matter where in the world you are.
